Thunder 380 Plus

Through its history, the Thunder 380 pistol has been able to impress who require concealability, reliability, and accuracy. On top of that, we added high capacity to the model.

In addition to the attributes of the traditional model 380 Plus, you will find renewed details such as: its lighter frame, its new magazine catch, more safety in the operation through its firing pin safety and its integral locking system.

Its frame is made of an aluminium alloy offering not only an excellent strength but also reduced weight, which leads to a superbly balanced pistol.

Thunder 40 Pro

The Thunder 40 PRO is the perfect choice for those looking for a gun with the stopping power of a 40 S&W.

Conceived as a law enforcement and military service weapon, you can depend on this gun for service, self defense or sports.

Its capacity, accuracy and reliability are an excellent combination for a pistol. They also became a good choice in sport disciplines as IPSC and IDPA.

It is an ergonomically excellent gun. Every single detail in its design has been well cared to for providing comfort and safety. This model is 100% ambidextrous since its safety levers and slide stop levers are located on both sides of the grip, and its magazine catch is also reversible.

The Thunder PRO's polygonal rifled barrel offers an excellent performance and durability, and a new firing pin design provides an extremely durable life for a service pistol.

The location of the safety and decocker grant unequalled comfort and a perfect grip.

The Thunder 40 PRO has the Improved Browning Petter barrel locking system, giving an excellent reliability during ejection of empty cases and feeding live ammo.

As we always do, safety is first, and Thunder 40 PRO has many safety devices: manual safety lever and decocker, firing pin safety, hammer automatically remains in half cock notch and separate the hammer from the firing pin, and a new loaded chamber indicator.

FINISHES: matte blue. It also comes in two tones with nickel plated slide and an anodized frame for superficial hardening and corrosion resistance.

REAR SIGHT: Dovetail fixed, with white dots, u or bar for faster target acquisition.

FRONT SIGHT: fixed in the slide with white dot for faster target acquisition.

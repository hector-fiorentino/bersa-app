Thunder 9 Pro XT

The new BERSA THUNDER 9 PRO XT was conceived by and for Sport Shooters.

The Thunder 9 PRO XT adds improvements and new features as the result of the experience and relationship with these professionals and has been tested under the most adverse conditions showing its accuracy and dependability always.

It is an ergonomically excellent gun. Every single detail in its design has been well cared to for providing comfort, safety and an extreme accuracy.

Specs
Caliber 9x19 mm
Opertation semi-automatic
Total length 210 mm
Height 142 mm
Width 38 mm
Barrel length 126 mm
Weight 962 grs
Magazine 17 cartuchos
Barrel Grooving 6 grooves, twist: on turn in 254 mm
Finish Duo tone
Action double and single action with an external hammer
